A sultry summer's night illuminated a passionate tryst between two alluring women. They entwined their fervent limbs, as they caressed each other with an irrepressible longing - the skin of one blazingly hot against the other's seductively cool. Subsuming themselves in the moment, they experienced ecstatic rapture unparalleled by any alternative reality. Gnawing at their emotional depths was a barely-concealed understanding that what they were experiencing wasn't entirely socially palatable, but it only served to heighten their insatiable yearning for intimacy. The room around them grew ever more enshrouded with feverish ardor until, finally, exhausted from pleasure, they lay limp and contented in each others' arms.
