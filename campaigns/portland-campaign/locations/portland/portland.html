Portland

Description

Portlland Is the primary home of the adventurers Arlo Hardrock, Andi Highwynd, Lionore, & Mahron Diate. It is a small fishing town that rests at the end of the mouth of a large valley. The next closest town is the city of Vancouver along the valley.
