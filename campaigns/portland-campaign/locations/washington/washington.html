Washington

Description

The Capitol City of Olympia from which the greater Kingdom of Washington is ruled by the Olympian Royal Family. Port Land lies just outside the influence of Olympia, however it would be powerless to oppose them in any kind of military confrontation. Vancouver lies on the border between the Greater Kingdom of Washington and the Oregon Coalition.
