Portland Campaign

Recent Campaign Sessions Log

Session 4 | DM David Kelley

Today we find our party, Arlo, Mahron, and Lumberjack returning with Andi's corpse to town. When they arrive, Arlo decides to go recover from this ordeal in his mountain home. Soon after the party's arrival Lionore's voyage ends and makes port. With Lionore learning of Andi's death and Mahron of the blight no longer targeting the church, they head to the town in the mouth of Portland's valley, Vancouver. On the road they encountered a gypsy camp and as they passed through, acquired a tag along by the name of Asriel. While in Vancouver, the party has learned from resident priest ,James Clover, of the recently destroyed local church, that an enemy with the title The Smiling Man has been destroying churches in the region. Due to the church and its people being destroyed, resurrecting Andi seemed to be a lost cause. While on the road returning to Portland, the uncle of Asriel appeared and handily retrieves his wayward nephew while resurrecting Andi. The far reaching effects of this resurrection have yet to be seen.
